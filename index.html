About The Forest Dweller

"The Forest Dweller" is an atmospheric comic that tells the story of a lone wanderer in a mystical forest. Through hauntingly beautiful visuals and an evocative narrative, it explores themes of nature, solitude, and discovery.

Created on paper with love and care, this comic invites readers to lose themselves in the dense foliage and secrets lurking beneath the canopy.
